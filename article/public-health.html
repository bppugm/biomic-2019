Public Health Symposium

Topics

Primary health care, metabolic syndrome, maternal, infant and child health, epidemiology, family planning, enviromental health, occupation and safety health, mental health, adolescent and young adults health, social determinants of health, health communication and health information technology, non communicable diseases, health economics, oral health, sexual and reproductive health, and related areas.

Publication

The selected papers will be submitted in the Malaysian Journal of Medicine & Health Sciences (Special Edition) indexed by Scopus.

Submission

If you are going to present your paper in this symposium, please make sure that you have done these following instructions:

  1. Register your account to EDAS
  2. Write your paper in the provided format
  3. Upload your paper to EDAS

If you still require further assistance, you can read our tutorials or contact us.
